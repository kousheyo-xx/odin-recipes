Oreo Milkshake

Description

A rich and creamy indulgence, the Oreo milkshake blends the classic taste of vanilla ice cream with the irresistible crunch of Oreo cookies. Each sip delivers a perfect balance of smooth, velvety texture and delightful cookie bits. Topped with whipped cream, crushed Oreos, and sometimes a drizzle of chocolate syrup, this milkshake is a cool, satisfying treat that brings the nostalgic flavor of cookies and cream to life in a chilled, drinkable form. Perfect for dessert or a sweet snack anytime.

Ingredients

  • 4-5 oreo cookies
  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • ½ to 1 cup milk (adjust based on desired thickness)
  • Whipped cream - for topping(optional)
  • Chocolate syrup(optional)
  • Extra oreo crumbs(for garnishing,optional)

Steps

  1. Take out 4–5 Oreo cookies, 2 cups vanilla ice cream, and ½ to 1 cup milk.
  2. Add vanilla ice cream, milk, and Oreo cookies to a blender.
  3. Blend for 30–45 seconds or until everything is smooth and creamy.
  4. Add a little more milk if it’s too thick.
  5. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream, chocolate syrup, and crushed Oreo crumbs, if desired.
  6. Serve with a straw or spoon and enjoy your chilled Oreo milkshake!
