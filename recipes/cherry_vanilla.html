Cherry Vanilla Milkshake

Description

The Cherry Vanilla Milkshake is a sweet, creamy treat that combines the smooth richness of vanilla ice cream with the bold, fruity flavor of ripe cherries. Each sip bursts with the nostalgic blend of sweet cherry syrup and classic vanilla, creating a perfect balance of tart and creamy. Blended to a silky consistency, this milkshake is often topped with whipped cream, a maraschino cherry, and a drizzle of cherry syrup for extra flair. It’s a delightful, retro-inspired drink that’s both refreshing and indulgent—ideal for dessert or a summer cool-down.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • ½ to 1 cup milk (adjust for thickness)
  • ½ cup pitted cherries (fresh or frozen) or 3–4 tbsp cherry syrup
  • 1–2 tsp vanilla extract (optional, for extra vanilla flavor)
  • Whipped cream (for topping – optional)
  • Maraschino cherry (for garnish)
  • Cherry syrup (for drizzling – optional)

Steps

  1. If using fresh cherries, remove pits and stems. You can also use cherry preserves or syrup if fresh cherries aren't available.
  2. In a blender, combine:
    • Vanilla Ice Cream
    • Milk
    • Cherries or Cherry Syrup
    • Optional: Vanilla Extract
  3. Blend for 30–45 seconds until creamy and pink. Add more milk if it’s too thick or more cherries for extra flavor.
  4. Pour into a chilled glass. Top with whipped cream, a maraschino cherry, and a drizzle of cherry syrup.
  5. Best served fresh and cold with a straw or spoon!
